About Me

I am currently an Assitant Professor (RTDa) in the Department of Information Engineering and Computer Science at Univeristy of Trento, Italy. I was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in CVLab at EPFL, working with Prof. Pascal Fua and Dr. Mathieu Salzmann. I received Ph.D Degree from Univeristy of Trento, Italy. My supervisor was Prof. Nicu Sebe and I was co-supervised by Prof. Yan Yan I was a visiting student from 2015 to 2016 in the Learning and Vision (LV) Group at National University of Singapore (NUS), working with Prof. Shuicheng Yan and Prof. Jiashi Feng.

Research Interests

Face analysis, human action understanding, augmented reality (AR), segmentation, optimization problems, etc.
