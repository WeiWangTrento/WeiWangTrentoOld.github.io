Wei Wang 王伟

I received my Ph.D Degree in Multimedia and Human Understanding Group MHUG at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Univeristy of Trento, Italy. My supervisor was Prof. Nicu Sebe, and I was co-supervised by Doc. Yan Yan. My research area is deep learning and its applications in computer vision and multimedia. Now I'm working as a postdoc in CVLab at EPFL, working with Prof. Pascal Fua and Dr. Mathieu Salzmann. During my Ph.D study, I was fortunate to visit Learning and Vision group, National University of Singapore in 2015 and 2016, working with Prof. Shuicheng Yan and Prof. Jiashi Feng.

 

 

Research

I am interested in face analysis (face landmark localization, face aging, expression analysis), human action understanding (multiview action recongnition) and hand segmentation.
